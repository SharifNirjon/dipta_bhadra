Numerical Analysis of Hydrodynamic Impact of 2D Bow-Flare Ship Structure with Varying Roll Angle

Dipta Bhadra, Md. Mashiur Rahaman, Syed Siddik Shouvik

This study builds an OpenFOAM model of vertical water-entry slamming for 2D wedge and bow-flare sections across multiple roll angles using VOF free-surface capture, dynamic overset meshing, and a transient PIMPLE scheme. Results are validated against experimental data.
