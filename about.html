About Me

I graduated with a B.Sc. in Naval Architecture & Marine Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in March 2025, achieving a CGPA of 3.62/4.00.

My academic journey has been defined by a deep interest in computational approaches to fluid problems — from water-entry slamming simulations to offshore wind turbine aerodynamics. I am proficient in OpenFOAM, ANSYS Fluent, Star CCM+, Python, MATLAB, and C++, and I have hands-on experience applying VOF free-surface methods, dynamic overset meshing, and virtual PMM simulations.

I am now seeking a PhD position in the USA to push the boundaries of CFD and numerical methods in ocean and marine engineering.
