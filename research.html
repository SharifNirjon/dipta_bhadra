Research & Projects

Nov 2023 – Mar 2025 | Supervisor: Prof. Dr. Md. Mashiur Rahaman, BUET Under Review

Numerical Simulation of 2D Hydrodynamic Impact of Wedge and Bow-Flare Section with Roll Angle (Undergraduate Thesis)

Built an OpenFOAM model of vertical water-entry slamming for 2D wedge and bow-flare sections across multiple roll angles, using VOF free-surface capture, dynamic overset meshing, and a transient PIMPLE scheme to resolve jetting and pressure peaks. Validated results against experimental data through pressure-time histories, impact loads, and velocity profiles.

Publication: "Numerical Analysis of Hydrodynamic Impact of 2D Bow-Flare Ship Structure with Varying Roll Angle" — Dipta Bhadra, Md. Mashiur Rahaman, Syed Siddik Shouvik
Tools Used: OpenFOAM, VOF Method, Dynamic Overset Meshing, PIMPLE Scheme
Nov 2022 – Aug 2023 | Supervisor: Prof. Dr. Md. Shahjada Tarafder Completed

Design of a 500-Passenger Vessel for the Dhaka–Patuakhali Route

Completed end-to-end preliminary vessel design including route analysis, principal particulars sizing, general arrangement layout, and lines plan fairing. Performed hydrostatic and intact stability checks, resistance and power estimates, structural scantling calculations, weight determinations, and produced detailed 3D models and a comprehensive design dossier.

Tools Used: Rhinoceros, AutoCAD, SolidWorks, stability & resistance calculation methods
Independent Research Ongoing

CFD Analysis of Hydrofoil Design for Improving Planing Hull Efficiency

Optimizing the Sleig2312 hydrofoil for high-speed planing hulls using ANSYS and OpenFOAM. Research focuses on identifying the angle of attack that minimizes drag and improves overall hull efficiency.

Tools Used: ANSYS Fluent, OpenFOAM
Independent Research Ongoing

Numerical Investigation of Aerodynamic Performance of Offshore Wind Turbine in Low-to-Moderate Wind Regimes of Bangladesh

Investigating offshore wind turbine aerodynamics for Bangladesh's wind regimes using QBlade for blade section optimization and OpenFOAM for CFD validation of feasibility and performance.

Tools Used: QBlade, OpenFOAM
Independent Research Ongoing

Numerical Estimation of Ship Maneuvering Derivatives via Virtual PMM Simulations

Performing virtual Planar Motion Mechanism (PMM) simulations using OpenFOAM to extract linear and non-linear hydrodynamic derivatives and assess maneuvering characteristics of a hull.

Tools Used: OpenFOAM, Virtual PMM
Feb 2024 Presentation

Navigating Fatigue: Managing Material Wear in Heavy Marine Transport Engineering

Used full-ship fatigue assessment practices to identify hotspot regions and fracture morphologies. Designed and executed an AH36 side-shell test under prescribed loading to characterize structural and material response.

Category: Poster Presentation
